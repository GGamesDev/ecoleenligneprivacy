# Privacy Policy — Ecole en Ligne

*Last updated: July 2026*

## In Summary

The app collects nothing. No data leaves your phone, except to connect to your school’s portal — exactly as if you opened its website in a browser.

There is **no intermediary server**, no account to create with us, no usage analytics, no advertising, and no trackers.

## What is Stored and Where

Everything stays on the device.

| Data | Location | Reason |
|------|----------|--------|
| Portal username and password | iOS Keychain (encrypted by the system) | The portal session expires after ~10 minutes; without them, you would have to re-enter the password constantly |
| Session cookies | iOS Keychain | Maintain connection between screens |
| Report cards, absences, documents | App’s local storage | Display data offline and avoid re-downloading every time the app is opened |
| Theme preference | Local storage | Remember light or dark mode |

Credentials are saved with the `WHEN_UNLOCKED_THIS_DEVICE_ONLY` option: they are only accessible when the device is unlocked, and are never included in an iCloud backup or transferred to another device.

## Who Receives Your Data

No one other than your school’s portal (`www*.ecoleenligne.be`, the number depending on the chosen school), to which the app connects directly via HTTPS with your credentials.

We receive nothing. We operate no servers.

## Notifications

Notifications are **local**: the app compares the portal data with the previous version stored on the device and shows an alert if something has changed. No remote notification token is created, and no message passes through a third-party service.

## Deletion

The **Logout** button immediately and permanently deletes:

- Credentials from the keychain
- Session cookies
- All cached data

Uninstalling the app has the same effect.

## Children

The app displays school information concerning minors, but it is intended for parents. This information comes from the school’s portal, remains their property, and is neither copied nor transmitted elsewhere.
